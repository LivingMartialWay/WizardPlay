Hydra
💧 Water 🔥 Fire

Your Elemental Personality

Sensitive, insightful, sociable, extroverted, careful, emotional, supportive, thoughtful, energetic, dominant, enthusiastic, active, impulsive, achieving, protective, nurturing, intuitive

The hydra is an aquatic serpent with acidic blood and the ability to breathe fire. They possess many heads and have regenerative powers; for every head chopped from its body, two more will grow, unless the neck is cauterized with metal and flame. Even though hydras are often seen as monstrous, they are actually friendly and socially smart.

They are naturally imaginative and empathetic. Hydras often feel emotionally insecure, which makes them very protective of their home and family. They want both emotional and physical security. Like other Water types, they can be changeable and moody, especially because of their need for security.

Hydras have a strong sense of community and a deep need to belong, not just to their family but also to a chosen group, such as friends, a guild, or colleagues. They dislike being alone, and even brief periods alone can leave them feeling tired, sad, or anxious. Because they want to feel at home with their people, they are very sensitive to opinions.

Hydras are dreamy and imaginative, but they also have the motivation and organization to turn their ideas into reality. They are usually the warmest, most sociable, and well-liked of all the types, but they are very sensitive to rejection. Luckily, they are rarely rejected because of their great social skills and good sense of humor.

Hydras combine two very different elements, Water and Fire, which can create tension in their personality. Water is the main element, and it relates to their emotions, intuition, reflectivity, and sensitivity. Water and Fire can complement each other: Fire reduces Water's tendency to excessive sentimentality, while Water takes the heat out of Fire's urgency.

The Fire side of hydras brings impulsiveness, passion, energy, enthusiasm, and ambition. It also gives the Water side the push it needs to get things done instead of just drifting. Hydras are interested in people, myths, dreams, and art. These interests come from their Water side, but their excitement to follow them comes from Fire.

Hydras are friendly and warm, and they are less likely to be overbearing than similar types. They can lead if needed, but they prefer to support others independently. They do best around people and are usually outgoing, cheerful, and lively. Like other Water types, hydras are emotional, sensitive, impressionable, and very intuitive.

Hydras are principally concerned with bringing people together and breaking down boundaries to harmonious exchange. They are eager to serve others and encourage people to help themselves. They have excellent diplomatic skills that can restore peace in tense situations. They hate conflict and will go to great lengths to avoid it.
